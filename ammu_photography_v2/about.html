My Story

Hi, I'm Ammu

Photography is more than just clicking a shutter; it's about freezing a moment in time so that the feeling lasts forever. For the past decade, I have dedicated myself to the art of visual storytelling.

My style blends cinematic artistry with candid, authentic emotions. I believe in letting moments unfold naturally — observing the quiet interactions, the joyous tears, and the genuine laughter that makes your story unique.

Whether it's the chaotic beauty of a wedding day, the romantic anticipation of a pre-wedding shoot, or a dramatic personal portrait, my goal is to make you feel comfortable, beautiful, and truly seen.

Every session is a collaboration. I take the time to understand your vision, your personalities, and what makes your relationship unique — so that the final images feel unmistakably, beautifully yours.
